## 這是什麼

MusicHarbor 是一款追蹤音樂人動態的 app，連結你的 Apple Music 或 Spotify，當你關注的歌手有新單曲、新專輯或演唱會時，它會即時通知你。

## 優點

- 介面乾淨，資訊一目瞭然
- 支援 Apple Music 和 Spotify
- 追蹤音樂更新超方便：可追蹤喜愛的歌手、樂團，自動整理新專輯與單曲發布
- 介面簡潔直觀：設計乾淨，操作流暢，重點資訊一目了然
- 支援多平台整合：可連接 Apple Music、Spotify 等服務
- 通知即時：新歌、新專輯上線會即時提醒，不怕錯過
- 隱私友善：不需建立帳號即可使用大部分功能

## 缺點

- 部分功能需要付費解鎖
- Android 不支援：目前僅限 iOS 使用者
- 部分功能需付費：進階功能（如更多追蹤數量）需訂閱
- 資料偶有延遲：部分藝人更新可能不夠即時或完整
- 功能較單一：專注在「追蹤新音樂」，缺乏社群或推薦系統

## 使用心得

MusicHarbor 是一款讓人一用就回不去的音樂追蹤工具。對於平常習慣關注多位歌手或樂團的使用者來說，它最大的價值在於「幫你記住所有發行日」。過去常常需要分別打開 Spotify、社群媒體甚至新聞來確認新歌是否上線，但使用 MusicHarbor 後，所有新專輯、單曲與合作曲目都會被集中整理在同一個時間軸中，省時又清楚。

實際使用上，它的介面設計非常乾淨，幾乎沒有多餘的干擾。打開 App 就能看到近期與即將發布的作品，搭配通知功能，基本上不會錯過任何重要更新。與 Apple Music 或 Spotify 的整合也讓體驗更加流暢，看到新歌後可以直接跳轉播放，整體流程相當直覺。

不過，MusicHarbor 的定位也很明確——它不是用來「聽音樂」或「探索新歌」的工具，而是專注在「追蹤」。因此如果期待像推薦演算法或社群分享這類功能，可能會覺得稍嫌不足。此外，免費版在追蹤數量上有一定限制，對於關注大量藝人的重度使用者來說，可能需要考慮付費升級。

整體而言，MusicHarbor 是一款功能單純但實用性極高的 App。只要你有固定追蹤的音樂人，它就能大幅降低資訊分散帶來的困擾，是日常聽音樂流程中非常值得加入的一環。

MusicHarbor is one of those rare apps that quietly becomes indispensable if you care about staying up to date with your favorite artists. Instead of trying to be another streaming service, it focuses on a single job: tracking new releases—and it does that extremely well.

The core idea is simple. You connect your Apple Music or Spotify account, and MusicHarbor builds a personalized feed of upcoming and newly released music from artists you follow. What makes it stand out is how cleanly and reliably it delivers this information. Releases are organized chronologically, easy to browse, and rarely cluttered with irrelevant content.

The interface is minimal but thoughtfully designed. It doesn’t overwhelm you with features, yet it still offers useful filters like albums, singles, collaborations, and even music videos. There’s also a nice sense of control—you can fine-tune which artists appear and how notifications behave, which helps avoid the noise that similar apps often struggle with.

One of MusicHarbor’s biggest strengths is how it complements, rather than competes with, your streaming service. It doesn’t try to replace discovery algorithms; instead, it ensures you never miss releases from artists you already care about. For many listeners, that alone solves a real problem.

There are a few limitations. The free version is somewhat restricted, and some of the more advanced filtering and customization options are locked behind a subscription. Also, if you’re looking for deep music discovery or editorial content, this isn’t the app for that—it’s intentionally focused.

Overall, MusicHarbor excels by doing one thing exceptionally well. If you’ve ever felt like new releases slip past you unnoticed, this app is a simple, elegant solution that earns its place on your home screen.
